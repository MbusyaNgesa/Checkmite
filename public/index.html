Chess, often dubbed the "Game of Kings," is a timeless classic that has captivated minds for over a millennium. Its origins trace back to the 6th century in India, where it was known as "Chaturanga," a game that mirrored the four divisions of the Indian army. From there, it traveled through Persia, where it became "Shatranj," and eventually spread to the Islamic world and medieval Europe, evolving into the game we recognize today.
